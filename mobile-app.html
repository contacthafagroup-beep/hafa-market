Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free to download?
Yes! Hafa Market is completely free to download and use. No subscription fees. You only pay for the products you order.
Which countries is the app available in?
Currently available in Kenya, Ghana, Nigeria, Ethiopia, Senegal, and expanding to more African countries. Check the app for your city coverage.
What payment methods are supported?
We support M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Credit/Debit Cards, PayPal, and Cash on Delivery. More payment options are being added regularly.
How fast is delivery?
Standard delivery is 24–48 hours. Express delivery (same day) is available in select cities. You can track your order live on the map.
Can I sell my farm products on the app?
Absolutely! Register as a seller, verify your identity, and start listing your products. You'll get access to 50,000+ buyers and a full seller dashboard.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! You can browse previously loaded products, view your cart, and check order history offline. The app syncs automatically when you reconnect.
What languages does the app support?
The app supports English, አማርኛ (Amharic), Afaan Oromoo, Kiswahili, Français, and العربية. More languages coming soon.
How do I return a product?
Go to My Orders, select the order, tap "Return Item" and follow the steps. We have a 7-day return policy with full refunds processed in 3–5 business days.
